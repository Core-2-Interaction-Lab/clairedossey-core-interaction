Light Collective

This collection is a love letter to light. I am fascinated with the shapes and colors made by direct light rays and the projection onto objects. For my main page I have all my pictures located with data I feel is useful in contributing to the way the light presents itself. I filtered my colors by hue and assigned each hue a found audio I felt represented that hue. For instance, pink is a soft and lonely color to me so I put the audio I recorded from my window of my neighbors signing “I get misty”.
