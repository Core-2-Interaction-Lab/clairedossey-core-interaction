            The div container groups a section of content together and puts padding around it
            to hold it in a uniform way. 
            The article tab holds the content together and allows the content to be more
            managable and organized.
            class= "wrapper main about" defines the padding between the negative space above
            the start of the article and the article.
            class= "block text-center" creates space for an object, thus holds the picture 
            in a fixed position.
            class= "block story" contians the artical's text and centers and positions the text.
